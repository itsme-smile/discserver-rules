GlowStream's Server Rules

1. Be respectful to all members.
2. No bullying or disrespect.
3. Keep it safe for work (no NSFW content).
4. No spamming or flooding chat.
5. Keep discussions in the correct channels.
6. No self-promotion anywhere.
7. Respect mods and admins.
8. Follow Discord ToS and Community Guidelines.
9. Do not share private information.
10. No loud or disruptive noises in VC.
11. No drama or toxic behavior.
12. Use appropriate usernames and avatars.
13. English language only unless specified.
14. No mini-modding (let staff handle problems).
15. No impersonating staff or other members.
16. No illegal activities or discussions.
17. Keep memes and media appropriate.
18. No server raiding or threats.
19. Don’t abuse bots or commands.
20. Respect everyone’s opinion.
21. No harmful, offensive, or discriminatory behavior.
22. Don’t ghost ping others.
23. No talking over streamers in VC.
24. Don’t ask for free giveaways repeatedly.
25. Use spoiler tags when needed.
26. Keep politics and religion out of chat.
27. Respect streamers’ or YouTubers’ recording sessions.
28. No copy-pasta spam.
29. Music bots only in music VCs.
30. Content must follow YouTube policies if linked.
31. No ear-rape or mic spamming.
32. Handle disputes privately with staff.
33. No bypassing word filters.
34. Stay positive and friendly!
35. Give constructive feedback.
36. Respect everyone’s time in events or games.
37. Do not leak or record without permission.
38. Voice changers are allowed only if not disruptive.
39. Have fun and support each other!
40. To report any issues, head to #report.
41. Need assistance? Visit #support for help.
